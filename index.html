Approximately 8 minutes

Real-life scenarios that reveal how your child responds when things go wrong, when they need to lead, or when they must collaborate under pressure. Decision-making instincts are one of the clearest windows into professional character and natural strengths.
Approximately 7 minutes
How It Works

The science finds the stream.
Then it finds the career within it.

Sahirah.in works in two precise stages — first identifying which stream your child's mind is truly built for, then going deeper to find the specific career path that fits them best within that stream.

Stage 01

Stream identification

Aptitude depth, personality traits, interests and values combine to generate a stream probability score across all four Indian education streams.

Humanities — leadership, verbal, creative
Commerce — numerical, strategic, enterprising
Medical — biological, precise, service-led
Non-Medical — logical, spatial, systems-oriented
Stage 02

Career path within the stream

Once the stream is confirmed, the evaluation goes deeper — distinguishing between careers that share the same stream but demand entirely different minds.

Non-Medical: Deep Tech (CS/AI) vs Applied (Civil/Mech)
Medical: Core (MBBS/BDS) vs Allied (Pharma/BioTech)
Humanities: Civil Services vs Design vs Law vs Research
Commerce: CA / Finance vs MBA vs Entrepreneurship
