Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your data

1. What We Collect

  • Child's name, age, class, city, school name
  • Parent / guardian name, email address, mobile number
  • Psychometric test responses (185 questions)
  • Discovery question answers (12 questions at registration)
  • Payment transaction ID (we do not store card or UPI details — all payments processed securely by Razorpay)
  • Device type and browser (collected automatically)

2. How We Use It

  • To generate your child's personalised psychometric report
  • To send login credentials, test reminders, and receipts
  • To improve our question bank and scoring accuracy over time
  • We never sell your personal data to any third party
  • We never share individual data with schools or advertisers

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All data stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL, hosted on AWS)
  • All connections encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms — we cannot see or recover your plain-text password
  • Reports are accessible only to the registered parent or guardian

4. Children's Privacy

  • We collect a child's data only with explicit parental consent
  • Registration must be completed by a parent or guardian
  • Parents may request complete deletion of all data at any time

5. Your Rights

  • Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request full deletion of your account and associated data

To exercise any of these rights, email: contact@sahirah.in

6. Local Storage

  • We use browser localStorage to maintain your session
  • No advertising cookies or third-party tracking scripts
  • Clearing your browser data will log you out
